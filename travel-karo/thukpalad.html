Thukpa: Thukpa is called in Tibetan language that means noodles. The dish was originated in the eastern part of Tibet. The dish is also popular in Bhutan, Sikkim, Arunachal Pradesh and Ladakh. Thukpa Cuisine is a noodle soup that is prepared with chicken or vegetables. There are various types of Thukpa Recipe in Tibetan like Thenthuk, Gyathuk, Pathug, Drethug. It is the most popular Ladakh Cuisine.
