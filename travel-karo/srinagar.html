Srinagar


When in Kashmir you have to start your day with the famous Kashmiri chai called the Kahwah. Mildly spiced, especially with the invigorating flavours of cinnamon, cardamom, saffron and sweetened with honey, plus topped with nuts, Kashmiri Kahwah is not just a beverage - it is a tradition, a sip of heavenly Kashmir itself! Usually served with the famous multi course meal (Wazwan) in which there are close to 20 -40 dishes served. The most popular among them being Rogan Josh, a lamb dish. There are almost nine kinds of breads in Kashmir like katlams, kulchas and bakirkhani among others. The sheer chai with a tinge of salt and spices is something you should try out.

If you are a vegetarian then the Dum Aloo is an all-time favourite, a celebrastion is Kashmir is incomplete without the inclusion of Dum Aloo in its menu. Other popular vegetarian dishes are Rajma Gogji or Rajma Shalgam red kidney beans cooked with turnips, flavoured with aniseed and ginger powder, the exotic Gogji Nadir turnips with lotus stem and the Chokh Vangun, a sour, brinjal curry.
