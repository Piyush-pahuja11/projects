Local Food and Festivals

A delectable combination of Punjabi and Tibetab is what Mashobra’s delicious local cuisine is all about and you cannot leave without sampling it. The robust meat dishes are seasoned with plenty of local spices including red chillies, cinnamon, cloves and cardamom. Try the popular regional favorites Kullu trout, grilled fish and Chicken Anaardana. Vegetarians have reasons to rejoice too. There is a variety of dishes for you to choose from. Start with the local specialities like Sepu Vadi, Guchhi Mattar and Kaddu Ka Khatta. Or indulge in Dham, rice cooked with green lentils, red kidney beans and yogurt. End on a sweet note with the Boor Kikari, a rice based sweet dish. Forget calorie counting and make the most of the culinary goodness on offer.
